CANDU Plant Control Systems

The Evolution Continues

L3 MAPPS is a leading global supplier of control and simulation solutions for the marine, power generation and space sectors.

Headquarters: Montréal, Canada

Number of Employees: Approximately 350 worldwide

Worldwide Offices: L3 MAPPS has operating units located in Canada, the United States, the United Kingdom, India and Malaysia. More information on L3 MAPPS international locations, click here.

Business Areas:

Marine Systems and Simulation
Naval vessel control systems, land-based and on-board simulators and turnkey training solutions.

Power Systems and Simulation
Real-time power plant simulators and simulator modernization for all plant types and CANDU plant computer systems.

Space Systems and Simulation
Real-time simulators for robotic systems and satellites to support simulation-based design and operations/maintenance training.
